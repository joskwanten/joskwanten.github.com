TsMsx - Browser-Based MSX Emulator

Welcome to TsMsx, a nostalgic journey back to the world of MSX computers right from your web browser. Crafted with passion and written in TypeScript, TsMsx allows you to relive the magic of MSX 1 emulation, offering a glimpse into the computing era that fueled our digital adventures.

About

TsMsx is not just an emulator; it's a personal ode to the past. The MSX, my first computer, bestowed upon me as a cherished 13th birthday gift by my parents, holds a special place in my heart. With TsMsx, I invite you to share in the nostalgia and experience the joy of MSX computing.

Features

  • Browser-Based: Run TsMsx directly in your web browser, eliminating the need for additional installations. Enjoy the MSX experience with ease, anytime, anywhere.
  • MSX 1 Emulation: TsMsx faithfully recreates the MSX 1 environment, allowing you to run your favorite MSX 1 games and applications.
  • Written in TypeScript: Leveraging the power and flexibility of TypeScript, TsMsx is not just an emulator; it's a testament to the modern development tools that bring the past to life.
